Favourte Film Ranking by D Subodh

Top 5 Favourite Films

1. Whiplash

A musical drama directed by Damien Chazelle portraying a young talent's struggle to become a great drummer.

2. Adventures of Tintin

An action adventure directed by Steven Spielberg showing a young man and his companion on a treasure hunt.

3. Spiderman 2

A classic superhero film by Sam Raimi showing the origin story of Spiderman's arch enemy Doc Ock.

4. Pinocchio

A Disney classic telling a story about a wooden puppet who wishes to be a real boy.

5. Schindler's List

A historical drama directed by Steven Spielberg showing the atrocities in Nazi Germany and how a Nazi industrialist aims to save as many Jews as he possibly can.
